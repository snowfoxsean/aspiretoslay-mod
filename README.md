# AspireToSlay — Slay the Spire 2 Mod

> **Automatically upload and track your Slay the Spire 2 runs at [aspiretoslay.com](https://aspiretoslay.com)**

[![License: MIT](https://img.shields.io/badge/License-MIT-yellow.svg)](LICENSE)

A Slay the Spire 2 mod that uploads and process your run files to provide insights on your gameplay.

- **No gameplay changes** — `affects_gameplay: false`
- **Standalone** — no other mods or libraries required
- **Privacy-first** — only your own account can see your data

Go to AspireToSlay.com/GettingStarted for more details
